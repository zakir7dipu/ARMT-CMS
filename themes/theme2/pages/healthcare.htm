Expanding Access to health Care

Abdur Rahim Memorial Trust is an organization working on a plethora of projects aimed at improving the quality of life of those who desperately need such assistance, the trust has expanded from its humble beginnings to become a reliable charity organization.


It’s an initiative by the five owners of Super Star Group for the memory of their late father Mr. Abdur Rahim.


Abdur Rahim Health Care Center is situated in the remote village of Patdal, under the Bhumkhara union of Naria Upazila in Shariatpur. Residents of this remote village have to face a lot of problems to get proper health care services. Abdur Rahim Health Care Center will be the solution for the residents immediate demand for proper health care service.

Abdur Rahim Health Care Center is underway in Patdal featuring a wide array of facilities to help the residents of this remote village.This hospital could be the answer to the prayers of the caring, hardworking people of this small village. Abdur Rahim Health Care Center has been slated to feature both certain designated areas upon the premises and a number of important services.

Future Improvements

The aforementioned facilities are only the proposed initial attributes of the hospital, and there are many upgrades planned. These include converting the hospital to a 10-bed facility, employing trained doctors who can perform more complex operations such as C-Sections, adding ECG and X-ray facilities, a larger pathological lab with disease detection capabilities, and providing blood transfusion services.

Of the 1 Crore 15 Lakh estimated budget, roughly Tk 6,775,000 has been raised, with another TK 4,725,000 required. Should this requirement be met, the lives of not only the people of Patdal, but also those living in villages nearby will be transformed.

Abdur Rahim Health Care Center is a dream project from the owners of Super Star Group, in order to help and provide the proper health service to the residents of Patdal village. As it’s the village of their ancestors.
