Eliminating hunger

During the pandemic, people from every class suffered a lot. Especially the poor underprivileged people who suffered from the food crisis. In this dire situation, Super Star Group the patron of ARMT decided to come forward and share the same food that they provide for their regular employees. This initiative still continues and every month from SSG a great amount of food is distributed to the poor underprivileged people of the society.

Super Star Group the patron of ARMT always works for the well-being of underprivileged people of society. And in the future, it has a plan to make this happen on a very large scale.
