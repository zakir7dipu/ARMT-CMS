Relentless future for the Employees

At the beginning of the new year, Super Star Group (SSG) introduced "Nirvar Agami Policy" for its employees. Under this policy, in case of death or physical/mental disability of an employee working in the Super Star Group, he or his family will be paid a substantial portion of his salary every month for the next three years.

Policy Details:

Death of an employee while working or providing the service.
Physical or mental incapacity of an employee caused while working or providing the service.
The employee will be paid a substantial part of his salary every month for the next three years (in case of physical or mental incapacity of an employee caused while working or providing the service).
The employee's family will be paid a substantial portion of his salary every month for the next three years in deceased of the employee (in case of the death of an employee while working or providing the service).

The policy is applicable for Drivers, Workers, Field Officers, Senior Field officers, and Service Assistants if the employment period is a minimum of 1 year.
The policy is applicable if the service period is a minimum of 2 years for the officers and the senior officers.

Under this policy, because of the sudden death of three employees Mohammad Waliullah (Senior Field Officer), Hasibul Hossain Shanto (Field Officer), and Kabir Hossain (Helper) Honorable Managing Director of Super Star Group Harun Ar Rashid handed over a cheque of the bulk amount their first month's salary to their respected families, which will continue for the next three years.
