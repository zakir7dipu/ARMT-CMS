About

In our imperfect society, there will always be those in need. While some people find it acceptable to ignore their often inaudible cries for help, others will go many extra miles to ensure a better future for all.

Abdur Rahim Memorial Trust is one such organization. Working on a plethora of projects aimed at improving the quality of life of those who desperately need such assistance, the Trust has expanded from its humble beginnings to become a reliable charity organization. It’s an initiative by the owners of Super Star Group for the memory of their late father Mr. Abdur Rahim.

ARMT’s projects include and have plan to go for ambulance and hearse services, disaster relief services, safe water, food, and winter clothing drives for the underprivileged, and scholarships for bright young minds that need the right nurturing to blossom into shining stars.

Vision

Help the People In need.

Mission

To go many extra miles to ensure healthy and meaningful life for all.

Goal

  • To ensure Safe Water & Food

  • To provide Health Care Service

  • To offer Ambulance and Hearse Services

  • To the uplift quality of life

Safe Water Projects

There is perhaps no curse greater than that of having to live with thirst, and ARMT aims to see a world where no person has to remain parched. With 57 pumps installed across Bangladesh, the trust has brought fresh, potable water to countless people across the country. And lots of projects are ongoing to ensure safe water in every district of Bangladesh.

Food Drives

Only those who have faced starvation truly understand the pain of hunger. ARMT’s goals include saving people from this cruel fate, and they have been organizing regular drives to provide both hot cooked meals and supplies for those living in extreme poverty. This has been especially important given the context of the pandemic when countless families have lost their sources of income.

Health for Those in Need

A clean bill of health is all that most of us ask for, yet few of us get. Illnesses, conditions, and accidents are part and parcel of life, and when they strike, one can only hope that medical care is accessible. Ensuring health facilities in remote villages is another mission of ARMT.

Ambulance and Hearse Services

It is a well-known fact that lots of underprivileged people come to the Capital city for better treatment and sometimes they have to face death long away from their own home and it becomes quite impossible for them to bring the dead body into their own home as a dead body carrying ambulance is quite expensive. ARMT’s reliable Ambulance and Hearse Services have been trying to combat this by providing the service for free for the people who are in need.

Despite having limited means, ARMT is doing its utmost to create a better world with its extensive network. The organization hopes to one day nullify all the inequalities that put people in situations where they need such help.
